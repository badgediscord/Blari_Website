Welcome to Blari Adventure

You play as a little boy named Blari and you have to survive throughout this adventure and encountered several pngs :)

Features

Mode Survival

In this mode you have to make it survive in order to complete the main adventure of young Blari!

Mode Online

In this mode, you can do whatever quests the pngs have in store for you in multiplayer!

Mode Creative

In this mode you can create your own personalized adventure and play it with your friends!
